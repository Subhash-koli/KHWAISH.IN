About Khwaish

KHWAISH is the annual inter-collegiate cultural festival hosted by the Computer Science Department (CSC). This two-day celebration of creativity and talent will take place on December 20th and 21st, 2024, featuring over 25 exciting events across diverse categories such as Fine Arts, Performing Arts, Star Events, Media Events, Sports, and more.

As Khwaish enters its fifteenth year, it continues to embody the spirit of youth aspiration. Our mission is to inspire and motivate students to push their limits, discover their true potential, and dream big.

This year promises to be bigger and better, as we are all set to welcome a vibrant crowd and elevate the festival to unprecedented heights.

About EK MELA

Welcome to Ek Mela, the theme of Khwaish College Fest 2024, where we bring the soul and joy of India’s traditional fairs right to our campus! This year, Khwaish is more than a fest—it’s a journey through the vibrant, bustling atmosphere of a classic Indian mela, where every corner brims with colors, sounds, and a sense of togetherness.

Ek Mela is a celebration of unity in diversity, showcasing the beauty of India’s varied cultures through art, music, dance, food, and games. Walk through stalls bursting with handcrafted items, explore food corners offering regional delights, and participate in exciting activities, from folk performances to thrilling games. Each event and attraction is designed to capture the spirit of a true Indian fair, blending nostalgia with the energy of youth.
